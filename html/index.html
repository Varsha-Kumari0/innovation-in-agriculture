Van Mahotsav in SLIET

Our college recently celebrated Van Mahotsav with great enthusiasm and fervor. The aim of the event was to spread awareness about the significance of trees in our lives and to encourage students to take up tree plantation drives. The event began with a plantation drive, where students, faculty members, and staff planted saplings in the college campus. The Club had arranged for a variety of saplings, including fruit trees, medicinal plants, and ornamental plants, to be planted. The students were given a brief on the importance of each of the plants and how they contribute to the environment.

Tree Plantation

Tree plantation is a simple yet effective way to contribute to the well-being of our environment. Trees are not only aesthetically pleasing but also play a vital role in maintaining ecological balance. They purify the air and provide shade. Additionally, trees also help prevent soil erosion, support biodiversity, and provide habitat for a wide range of species. As a college community, we can come together and make a positive impact on our environment by planting trees on Independence Day. This activity can not only help improve our surroundings but also instill a sense of responsibility and pride among the students and staff.

Flower Show at SLIET

Flower shows are an excellent way to celebrate the beauty and diversity of flowers and plants. The flower show was not just a feast for the eyes but also a learning experience. Visitors had the opportunity to learn about the different types of flowers and their characteristics, as well as the various methods used for cultivating and propagating them.Overall, the flower show was a wonderful celebration of nature's beauty and diversity. It was an event that brought together people from different backgrounds and interests, united by their love for flowers and plants.
